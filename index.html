First Undisputed Lightheavyweight World Champion!

Artur Beterbiev became the first Undisputed lightheavyweight champion in history by beating Dmitrii Bivol in a divisive majority decision

Bivol was the first fighter who took Artur Beterbiev to a decision which many thought he won. There are already talks for a rematch

Artur Beterbiev has now beaten :

  • Dmitry Bivol 23-0
  • Callum Johnson 17-0
  • Oleksandr Gvozdyk 17-0
  • Joe Smith Jr. 28-3
